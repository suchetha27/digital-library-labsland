What is Remote Laboratory System?

• Remote Laboratory is a way to provide opportunity to conduct experiments in real time at there own pace, from anywhere and anytime

• The remote laboratories are used for experiential learning and will help to understand concepts by "Learn by doing"

Remote Laboratory System at ETE

• Remote Laboratory System for Analog Electronics

• More than 40 experiments,250 +variations

• Feature of Remote Laboratory System

• Guaranteed output more time circuit analysis

• Easy procedure

• Option to modify the signal parameter at users wish!!

• Sufficient pre-defined component variations

• Time and frequency response

• Measurement and doccumentation is easy

Objective

TO provide necessary support to conduct the experiments smoothly

• It includes

• Access information of the remote lab system

• Introductory videos of each experiment

• Analysis Videos

• Theoretical background

• Conduction procedure

• Sample report format
